Safety & Support

Everything You Need to Stay Safe

General Public Safety

badge

Always carry your accreditation; it is required for all permitted access zones.

payments

Avoid carrying large amounts of cash; keep small denominations for minor purchases.

atm

Only use ATMs located inside secure facilities such as malls or hotel lobbies.

local_taxi

Utilize official ride-sharing apps (Uber, Cabify) or authorized radio taxis dispatched by hotels.

visibility_off

Keep high-value electronics and jewelry out of sight when in public spaces.

Proactive Safety Measures

share_location

Share your daily itinerary and live location with a trusted contact or teammate.

contact_phone

Pre-save local emergency numbers and your hotel's address in your phone's contact list.

wifi_lock

Exercise caution with sensitive personal data when connected to unencrypted public Wi-Fi networks.
